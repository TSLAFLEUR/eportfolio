About Me:

Hello, my name is Tyler LaFleur. I was born and raised in San Diego, California where I started to gain an interest in computers. However, I had another calling and decided to join the United States Marine Corps in 2016, where I served as an infantrymen for 5 years. After serving two deployments overseas, I decided to separate from the Marine Corps and moved to Coeur D'Alene, Idaho, where I started to study Computer Science at North Idaho College. I have since then graduated with my Associates Degree and have continued to pursue a Bachelor's degree with University of Idaho.

Since I started, I have studied multiple computer languages including, but not limited to, C++, C#, C, python, and HTML to name a few. In addition, I also have conversational knowledge of Japanese, which I have been studying since 2018. Also, thanks to my background in the Marine Corps, I am also skilled in being able to not only listen and follow directions from clients, but I am also able to lead teams efforts. This allows me to be able to work effectively with others while being able to communicate efficiently.

Check out some of the projects that I've worked on, to include a Legend of Zelda inspired action adventure game created with Unity in C#, Duck Quest, a final fantasy-esque turn-based game Final Duck created in C++, a hangman game styled in Assembly Language, as well as a Shell program created in C.
